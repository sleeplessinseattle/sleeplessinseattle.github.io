If you would like to submit a donation to Sleepless in Seattle, please mail a check to "Urban Hands - 8420 Greenwood Ave N. Seattle WA 98103" with check addressed to "Urban Hands" memo "Sleepless in Seattle."

As Sleepless in Seattle is an entirely volunteer-driven grassroots initiative, 100% of donations goes directly towards buying sleeping bags in discounted bulk rates (less than $20/bag).

Additional sleeping bags will be distributed through grassroots events and non-profit partners primarily in King County and, if funds allow, in the greater WA state.
