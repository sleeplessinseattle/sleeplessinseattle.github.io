Sleepless in Seattle is a volunteer-led group based out of Seattle, WA committed to caring meeting, serving, and befriending those experiencing homelessness in King County with sleeping bags and a smile.

We will be hosting our 2017 Big Give on Saturday, December 16th from 2pm-8pm at six different locations around King County. Together, we will raise over $60,000 to purchase and distribute sleeping bags, mats, and care packages to every homeless person in King County with the help of 360 volunteers.
