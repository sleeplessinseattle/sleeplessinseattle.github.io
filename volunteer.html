Volunteer with Sleepless in Seattle 2015

Thank you for your interest in volunteering with Sleepless in Seattle!

Volunteer opportunities are available for people regardless of experience and qualifications. We ask only that any students younger than 16 years old come accompanied by an adult.

To get started, please fill out this volunteer signup form, and we will get back to you with a number of placement options including the Big Give on Saturday 11/21/2015.

As a volunteer, you will have the opportunity to:

  1. Serve those on the streets directly to meet immediate needs, provide emotional support and friendship, and refer individuals to appropriate social services.
  2. Be exposed to the reality of living on the streets of King County.
  3. Learn sensitivity and relational skills by serving side by side with experienced volunteers.

Looking for alternative ways to serve the homeless in King County? Take a look at our list of volunteer opportunities from non-profits around the community.
